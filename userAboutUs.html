About Us

Athfest Music & Arts Festival is produced by local non-profit Athfest Educates. Since 2009, AthFest Educates has contributed more than $125,000 to music and arts education programs for Athens-Clarke Country children.

In addition to our summer music festival, we also produce the AthHalf Half Marathon, our 2nd major fundraiser.
